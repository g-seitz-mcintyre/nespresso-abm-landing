Our point of view

Where we see the biggest opportunities for Nespresso.

We’re not proposing a brand reinvention. We’re proposing a more intelligent commercial system: stronger repeat behavior, sharper audience logic, more connected lifecycle journeys, and a clearer path from customer insight to measurable performance lift.

01 — Lifecycle orchestration

Increase customer value after the first conversion

There’s likely room to sharpen replenishment timing, upsell sequencing, bundling logic, and post-purchase journeys around machine ownership, taste profile, and buying cadence.

02 — Experience-to-performance alignment

Make premium brand experience and performance marketing reinforce each other

We often see premium brands treat brand and performance as separate tracks. The stronger model is integrated: media, landing experiences, CRM, and offer design all working from the same commercial logic.

03 — Strategic growth motions

Prioritize the channels and partner plays most likely to compound

Whether the lever is CRM, CRO, affiliate, strategic partners, or premium acquisition, the key is not doing more at once — it’s identifying which plays can create outsized commercial impact first.
