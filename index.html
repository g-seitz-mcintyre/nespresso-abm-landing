The ABM playbook

A four-part system for premium growth.

Built for a brand that already has presence, trust, and customer love — but wants a more surgical go-to-market motion across acquisition, retention, and strategic accounts.

Phase 1

Signal mapping

  1. Audit current audiences, lifecycle stages, and channel performance
  2. Identify premium-value signals and drop-off moments
  3. Prioritize high-impact customer and partner segments
Phase 2

Journey design

  1. Create modular messaging by audience and buying moment
  2. Align CRM, paid media, onsite content, and offer logic
  3. Design premium-safe test plans with measurable hypotheses
Phase 3

Activation

  1. Launch high-priority lifecycle and account-specific plays
  2. Coordinate creative, segmentation, and targeting rules
  3. Track movement from engagement to repeat revenue
Phase 4

Optimization

  1. Refine journeys based on behavioral and commercial data
  2. Scale winning plays across additional markets or segments
  3. Build an always-on experimentation cadence
